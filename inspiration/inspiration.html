Jenni Radisevich
She is one of my favorite DIY bloggers.

DIY (do-it-yourself) realm, with a popular blog

featured on the Marta Stewart Show, Rachel Ray Show,
CNN.com, The Today Show, Wall street Journal
and InStyle magazine. You can find DIY tips
and ideas on her blog.
